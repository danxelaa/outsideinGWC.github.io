About Us

Girls Who Code is a seven-week program that teaches girls from all over America how to code as to motivate more interest and diversity in the tech sector. Girls Who Code introduced us to a variety of programming languages that we later used to create our website during the last two weeks of the summer immersion program.
We live in a world where if you break your arm, everyone rushes to sign the cast, but if you tell someone you're depressed, everyone runs the other way or brushes it off as a "first world problem." Our goal with Outside In is to bring awareness to depression and end the stigma associated with mental illnesses.
